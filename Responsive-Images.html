We’re kicking off grilling season with this list of our favourite grilled chicken recipes because you deserve a summer full of the best, juiciest grilled chicken, whether you’re firing up the charcoal (or gas grill) to cook smoky wings, yogurt-marinated kebabs, super-flavourful chicken thighs, or the whole dang bird. Below you’ll find our favourite marinades and brush-on sauces, plus all the classic grilling methods, including chicken under a brick, butterflied chicken, beer can chicken, easy boneless chicken breasts, and more. Pair these grilled chicken recipes with slaw or salad, grilled vegetables, or simply sliced tomatoes—whatever you add to the menu, these chicken dishes may hog the spotlight. Just don’t forget to grill some brownies for dessert.
